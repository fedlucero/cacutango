Tango

Since 2002 teaching and researching in tango technique, from somatic, musicality, dynamics. Sharing my vision of dancing tango from a simple and pleasant way of going through it, trying to get you to your own dance as a starting point to share it with another person in an embrace. Also teaching and sharing classes of Milonga and Vals, with their technique and particularities. Very beautiful rhythms to learn and deepen.

Tango Contact

Since 2011 researching and teaching together with Roxana Umpierr and then with other friends, this new discipline born from the encounter of Argentine tango and Contact Improvisation. Bringing principles from both dances, we explore many new possibilities of dancing within an embrace, dancing with different musical pieces, dancing in trios, quartets, etc, adding tango steps to a free exploration brought by CI and so on. The result is a space of free movement and exploration fully supported by tango and CI technique, which bring great qualities to the dances. You are invited to bring your own colour to the dance.

Special Workshops

Unique, intense and in-depth studies on different aspects of Tango. Dimensions of musicality. A workshop divided into 4 parts, which can be done from 1 to 16 lessons (rhythm, musical parts, melody and musical lines, interpretation and playing). Power milonga. Rhythm, playful way of dancing, body games to achieve the movements, tango technique applied to milonga, qualities of muscle tone, anticipation, spatiality and more, are some of the topics we intend to study in this special workshop. Tango Clown. A charming fusion that I have been exploring since 2014. Among precepts and dynamics of the Clown Technique, fused with tango steps or tango concepts such as "abrazo", "la ronda", "el final". Playing, having fun, learning, exposing oneself, transcending forms... Do you want more information= Contact me!

Training

Pedagogical training for dance teachers Do you want to discover your own pedagogy? Or do you want to expand it? In this training, I propose 4 perspectives of study with games, exercises, technique and info about what could be a way to transmit a concept. Furthermore, we go through different questions about the purpose of a lesson, how to teach with a gender perspective, how to prepare a lesson and how to consider the conditions of the environment (space, population, experience, sound, history, etc.).
